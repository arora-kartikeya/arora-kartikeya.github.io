About Me

I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering at the University of Southern California, where I focus on the theoretical aspects of quantum computing. My research interests span quantum algorithms, quantum complexity theory, and quantum information processing.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical quantum computing concepts and practical implementations. Through my research and tutorials, I aim to make quantum computing more accessible to the broader scientific community.
