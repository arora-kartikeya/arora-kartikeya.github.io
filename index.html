About Me

I am a Physics PhD student at the University of Southern California, specializing in quantum computing and quantum information theory. My research focuses on developing novel quantum algorithms and exploring the fundamental principles of quantum mechanics in computational contexts.

With a passion for both theoretical physics and practical applications, I work at the intersection of quantum mechanics, computer science, and information theory to push the boundaries of what's possible in quantum computation.
