About Me

I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering at the University of Southern California, specializing in quantum computing theory. My research focuses on developing novel quantum algorithms and understanding the fundamental limits of quantum computation.

My work spans theoretical aspects of quantum information processing, including quantum complexity theory, quantum error correction, and the intersection of quantum computing with machine learning and optimization.
