Efficient Quantum Error Correction for NISQ Devices Using Adaptive Syndrome Extraction

K. Arora, J. Smith, M. Johnson | Physical Review A, 2024

Abstract: We present a novel approach to quantum error correction specifically designed for noisy intermediate-scale quantum devices. Our adaptive syndrome extraction method reduces the overhead associated with traditional error correction schemes while maintaining high fidelity for quantum computations.
