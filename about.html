About Me

Background

I am a PhD candidate in Electrical Engineering at the University of Southern California, specializing in quantum computing. My research focuses on developing novel quantum algorithms and error mitigation techniques for near-term quantum devices.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical quantum computing and practical implementations. My work aims to address the challenges of quantum noise and decoherence in current quantum processors while exploring new applications in machine learning, optimization, and simulation.

Research Interests

  • Quantum algorithms for optimization and machine learning
  • Error correction and mitigation for quantum systems
  • Quantum circuit optimization
  • Quantum software development
  • Quantum hardware characterization
